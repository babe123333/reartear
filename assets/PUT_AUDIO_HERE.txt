Put your music files in this folder:

  liminal_site/assets/

Easiest way:

1) Rename your MP3 to:
   track.mp3

2) Refresh the site.

Optional (multiple tracks):
- Put: track-01.mp3, track-02.mp3, track-03.mp3 ...
- Then edit liminal_site/index.html and set:
  data-tracks="./assets/track-01.mp3, ./assets/track-02.mp3"
